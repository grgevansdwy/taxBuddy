Frequently asked questions

TaxBuddy is built specifically for non-resident aliens in the U.S., including international students on F, J, M, and Q visas, who need to file Form 1040-NR.
Generally, if you are an international student on an F-1 or J-1 visa and have been in the U.S. for less than 5 calendar years, you are considered a non-resident for tax purposes.
TaxBuddy is currently free to use as we want to help as many students as possible file correctly and easily.
Yes, we use bank-level encryption for all data transmission and storage. We never share or sell your personal information.
We support generating Form 1040-NR, Form 8843, and handle inputs from W-2, 1099-INT, 1099-DIV, and 1042-S.
Currently, the IRS has restrictions on e-filing 1040-NR for many users. We provide complete, filled-out forms ready to print and mail. Direct e-file support is coming soon.
